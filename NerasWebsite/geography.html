Our Lands

Neras sits in the beautiful forests surrounding other popular travelling towns. Neras is known for the lakes that rest next to it, and that seem to breathe with the winds winding through the trees and into this little piece of civilization we Nerasinians call "Home".


Our town itself feels cozy, dusted streets bustling with activity from peoples of many kinds, finding shelter beneath and within the trees. Past guests have loved their stay in Neras because it feels safe, and away from major cities, and offers a sweet, gentle quiet to relax and unwind to.
